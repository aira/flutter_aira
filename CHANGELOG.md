- **BREAKING:** The `Room` now manages its mute states separately from the `MediaStreamTrack`s (#35).

   ```dart
   _localMediaStream.getAudioTracks()[0].enabled = false;
   ```

   ```dart
   _room.setAudioMuted(true);
   ```
